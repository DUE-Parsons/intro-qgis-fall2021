Exercises

These exercises supplement Methods 1: GIS.

Finding data

  1. Go to NYC Open Data or Google dataset search.
  2. Search for data, could be something of use to you in a class or just something interesting to you. You won't need to use this later so feel free to explore.
  3. Try to figure out who maintains the data and see if there is any other metadata for the data.
